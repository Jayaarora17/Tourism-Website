Jammu :- The Tourist Attraction

Jammu is one of the largest cities of Jammu and Kashmir. The winter capital of the state is also known as Jammu Tawi and the City of Temples. The terrain is hilly and mountainous. The Pir Panjal Range separates it from the Kashmir Valley and the Himalayas in the east. Jammu is the main economic center of the area. Jammu lies on the banks of the pristine Tapi river. It is dotted with hundreds of temples. From the popular Hindu pilgrimage of Vaishno Devi, which is located nearby, to scores of religious structures set in picturesque surroundings, Jammu is the ideal site to set on a temple trail and soak in spiritual vibes.
